# FAQ Page Prompt Template

Use this prompt to generate an FAQ section for Ikerd Law Firm.

---

## Prompt

```
Write an FAQ section for Ikerd Law Firm (Lafayette, Louisiana) about [TOPIC/PRACTICE AREA].

Context: [ADDITIONAL INFORMATION ABOUT THE TOPIC - e.g., recent law changes, specific angles to cover, client notes, or relevant details]
Number of questions: [5-15 or specify]

Before writing, research and determine the best primary keyword and 3-5 secondary keywords for this topic based on search intent, relevance to Lafayette, Louisiana, and the practice area. List the chosen keywords at the top of your output before the content.

Follow all rules in CLAUDE.md. Specifically:
- No contractions, no em dashes, no cliche phrases
- No outcome guarantees
- Professional and compassionate tone
- Verify all Louisiana statutes and legal information for accuracy
- Write questions in the exact language a potential client would use when searching online

Deliver the following:
1. FAQ content with clear question-and-answer format
2. JSON-LD FAQPage schema markup for all Q&A pairs
3. Meta title (under 60 characters)
4. Meta description (150-160 characters with primary keyword and CTA)
5. Internal links to related practice area pages on ikerdlaw.com within answers
6. Include the standard disclaimer at the end
```
